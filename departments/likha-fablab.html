ABOUT LIKHA FABLAB

Likha FabLab is a state-of-the-art fabrication laboratory dedicated to advanced manufacturing and prototyping. Equipped with the latest in 3D printing, CNC machining, laser cutting, and electronics fabrication, the lab empowers researchers, engineers, and entrepreneurs to turn their ideas into reality.

Whether you're working on a research project, a startup prototype, or an industrial solution, Likha FabLab provides the tools and expertise needed to innovate.
