About CTI

The Center for Technopreneurship and Innovation (CTI) is a hub for aspiring entrepreneurs and startups. We provide mentorship, funding opportunities, and networking platforms to turn ideas into sustainable businesses.

CTI works closely with researchers, students, and industry professionals to drive innovation-driven entrepreneurship.
