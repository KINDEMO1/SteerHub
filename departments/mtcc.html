About MTCC

The Materials Testing and Calibration Center (MTCC) provides high-quality testing and calibration services to ensure materials meet industry standards. Our expert team works with researchers, engineers, and manufacturers to guarantee accuracy and compliance.

Equipped with state-of-the-art instruments, MTCC specializes in mechanical, chemical, and environmental testing, ensuring material durability and performance under various conditions.
